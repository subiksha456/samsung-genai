Mission 6 — How it works (read before running)

The function does 3 things after the model processes your prompt:

Step 1 — Logits
The model outputs one raw score for every word in the vocabulary (~32,000 words). Higher score = more likely to be the next word. These raw scores are called logits.
logits = outputs.logits[:, -1, :]

Step 2 — Softmax
Raw scores can be any number. Softmax converts them into probabilities between 0 and 1, all adding up to 1.0.
probs = torch.softmax(logits, dim=-1)

Step 3 — Top-K
Pick the 5 highest probability words. These are your top-5 predictions.
values, indices = torch.topk(probs, top_k)
