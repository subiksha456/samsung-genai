RAG vs. No-RAG Showdown

Same question, twice. Once the model guesses from memory, once it's grounded in a real document. This is the direct fix for what Lab 1's Hallucination Lab just showed you going wrong.

How this works

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) means giving the model the actual source document alongside your question, instead of trusting its training-time memory. Today you'll do the "retrieval" step by hand — copy-paste — so you can see exactly what changes in the model's answer when it has the real facts in front of it.
